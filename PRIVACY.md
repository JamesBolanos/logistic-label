## Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This application is designed to handle user information and, potentially, business data. We are committed to protecting your data and complying with applicable privacy regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant standards.

We collect and process the following types of information:
- User account information (such as email, username, and authentication data)
- Business data submitted by users (such as labels, documents, and related metadata)
- Usage data (such as access logs, device information, and interaction data)

## Purpose of Data Processing

We process your data for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain the core functionality of the application
- To authenticate users and secure access to business data
- To improve the application and user experience
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- All user and business data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
- Data is stored within the European Economic Area (EEA) or in jurisdictions with adequate data protection laws.

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell or rent your personal or business data to third parties.
- Data may be shared with service providers strictly for the purposes of application hosting, maintenance, or legal compliance, under strict confidentiality agreements.
- We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users or others.

## User Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct or update your data
- Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent at any time (where applicable)

## Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above or as required by law. Data will be securely deleted when no longer needed.
